Two Player Games

Memory See if you can remember the position of numbers in a grid. Earn more points than your opponent by matching more numbers. Memorise

Four In A Row Let circles fall into a grid by just dragging them. Make sure to align four same colored circles either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Fall to four
